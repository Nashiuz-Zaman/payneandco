About The Property

TRIPLE FRONTED PROPERTY!! A rare opportunity to acquire a prestigious, triple fronted, six bedroom, semi detached house located on The Drive in North Ilford within convenient walking distance to Valentines Park, Ilford town centre and mainline station with the oncoming Elizabeth Line and its major transport links. The property benefits from large living accommodation, double glazing, gas central heating, off street parking, private rear garden and can be configured in many ways to suit a large family. We would recommend an early internal inspection to avoid disappointment so please call our sales team for more information.
